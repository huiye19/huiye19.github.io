Hui YE (叶卉)

I am a RGC Postdoctoral Fellow at City University of Hong Kong (CityU). I obtained my PhD degree from the School of Creative Media, CityU, under the supervision of Prof. Hongbo Fu. My research interests lie in the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Computer Graphics (CG). Specifically, my main research focus is on exploring novel mobile AR prototyping tools and techniques for 3D contents and interactions. I closely collaborated with Dr. Kin Chung Kwan. Before I come to CityU, I received a BA degree in communication and a minor degree in computer science and technology from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2016.
